About Us

Welcome to
Pragati Polytechnic

Pragati Polytechnic is dedicated to providing vocational and employment-oriented training to career-conscious youth. Our mission is to assist young individuals in becoming independent through vocational education.

Established under the guidance of Smt. Lajwanti Educational Society, our aim is to empower the youth with the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in the professional world.

Our Mission

Progressive Values.
Practical Education.

Pragati Polytechnic is based on progressive values of education. It is always in the forefront to provide the latest and the best to its students. It maintains good rapport with professional organisations, commerce and industry.

The Polytechnic is committed to providing quality vocational and technical education so as to boost morale and instil new confidence — preparing students to accept the challenges of life and step forward into a successful career.
