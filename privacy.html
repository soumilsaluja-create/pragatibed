Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 July 2026  |  Pragati B.Ed Institute, Sector 8, Gurugram

Pragati B.Ed Institute ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who contact us or submit enquiry forms on our websites, including pragatibedinstitute.com and associated landing pages.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit an admission enquiry — whether through our website forms, WhatsApp, phone, or a Google Ads Lead Form — we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address (optional)
  • City or area of residence
  • Programme of interest (e.g., B.Ed, D.El.Ed, M.Ed, ITEP, NTT)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your admission enquiry
  • Provide information about programmes, fees, and eligibility
  • Schedule counselling sessions or campus visits if requested
  • Send relevant admission updates and deadlines

We do not use your information for unrelated marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information may be shared with internal admissions staff only, for the purpose of responding to your enquiry.

We use Formspree (formspree.io) to process web form submissions. Formspree's privacy policy governs their handling of data transmitted through their service.

4. Data Retention

Enquiry data is retained for up to 2 years for admissions record-keeping purposes, after which it is securely deleted.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct or delete your information
  • Withdraw consent for us to contact you at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: 98102 90953 or via WhatsApp at 98182 64953.

6. Cookies

Our website uses Google Analytics and Google Ads conversion tracking cookies to understand how visitors interact with the site. These cookies do not collect personally identifiable information directly. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.
